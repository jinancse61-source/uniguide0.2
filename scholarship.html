How to Apply for Scholarship

Simple steps to claim your scholarship

1
Submit Admission Application
Apply online at sub.edu.bd/admission with all required documents.
2
Automatic Scholarship Assessment
Merit scholarships are automatically assessed based on your GPA. No separate application needed.
3
Special Category Application
For Freedom Fighter / sibling / sports scholarships, submit a separate form at the Scholarship Office.
4
Receive Confirmation
Scholarship confirmation will be sent to your registered email within 7 working days of admission.
💡
Scholarship Renewal: Merit scholarships are renewable each semester provided the student maintains a minimum CGPA of 3.00 at SUB.
