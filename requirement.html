General Requirements

Applicable to all undergraduate programs

  • 1
    Combined GPA of 6.00 (SSC + HSC)
    No single subject grade below 2.50 in either exam.
    Min. 6.00
  • 2
    O-Level / A-Level Equivalence Accepted
    International curriculum students must submit equivalence certificate from BISE.
    Accepted
  • 3
    Age Limit: Maximum 25 years
    Calculated as of the first day of the enrollment semester.
  • 4
    Bangladeshi Nationals or Valid Visa Holders
    Foreign students must provide valid student visa documentation.
