Application Process

7 simple steps from start to enrollment

1
Create Account Online
Visit the official admission portal and register with your email address and mobile number.
🌐 sub.edu.bd/admission
2
Fill the Admission Form
Select your desired department and program. Enter SSC and HSC information, GPA, and personal details carefully.
⏱ Takes about 15 minutes
3
Upload Documents
Scan and upload SSC/HSC certificates, mark sheets, passport-size photo, and NID copy. Accepted formats: JPG, PNG, PDF.
📁 Max file size: 2 MB per document
4
Pay Application Fee
Pay BDT 500 application fee via bKash, Rocket, Nagad, or bank transfer. Keep the transaction ID safe.
💳 Fee: BDT 500 (non-refundable)
5
Submit & Download Slip
Review all entries, submit the form, and download the confirmation slip. You will receive an email confirmation.
6
Admission Test / Interview (if applicable)
If your selected department requires a test or interview, you will be notified via SMS and email with the schedule.
📞 Required for: Law, Architecture
7
Check Results & Enroll
Check the results on the portal. If selected, complete enrollment by paying semester fees and submitting original documents at the campus.
